Library

The library contains over 50,000 books and digital resources.The VIT Chennai Library (Periyar EVR Central Library) is a modern, automated facility supporting all VIT campuses, offering vast physical/e-resources (books, journals, e-books via "my off" app), RFID technology, digital library with touch tables, video conferencing, and dedicated study/discussion areas, operating with strict rules and biometric access, aiming to foster research and learning with continuous resource updates based on user needs.
